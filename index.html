What you can do in RecipeTube

Examples of real actions inside the app. These match features already implemented in your project.

Post recipes with media
Upload a recipe with ingredients + steps and attach photo process, video, or both.
Watch Reels
Scroll a full-screen 9:16 video feed with likes, comments, saves, and share.
Explore grid
Browse posts in an Instagram-style grid. Search shows accounts separately plus post results.
Chat with images and GIFs
Direct messages with text plus image and GIF uploads (and image/GIF links auto-render).
Follow, like, save
Follow creators, save recipes to your profile, and get recommendations based on likes.
Safety tools
Report posts/comments/users/ads, block users, and the server auto-blocks obvious spam.
